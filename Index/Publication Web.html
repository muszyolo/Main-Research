🤔 Journal vs. Conference Proceeding?

📘 Indexed Journal

  • ⏱️ 12–18 months total
  • 💪 Higher academic prestige
  • 📖 Deeper, complete research
  • ✅ Counts for MSc graduation
  • 💰 May have APC fees

🎤 Conference Proceeding

  • ⚡ 6–9 months total
  • 🌐 Networking opportunity
  • 🗣️ Present your work live
  • ✅ Counts for MSc graduation
  • 💰 Registration fee required
⭐ Recommended Strategy: Start with a conference proceeding for a quick win, then publish a full journal paper to expand the work.
