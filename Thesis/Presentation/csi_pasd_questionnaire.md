# 📋 CSI-PASD Questionnaire (Extracted from Chapter 4)

This instrument is the **Counseling Seeking Intention for Parents of Autistic Children (CSI-PASD)**. It is grounded in the Theory of Planned Behavior (TPB) by Icek Ajzen (2006) and has been validated for evaluating caregiver help-seeking intentions and behaviors.

---

## 📝 Survey Instructions

Please read each statement carefully and rate your agreement on a scale from **1 to 5**:
*   **1** = Strongly Disagree (Sangat Tidak Setuju)
*   **2** = Disagree (Tidak Setuju)
*   **3** = Neutral (Neutral)
*   **4** = Agree (Setuju)
*   **5** = Strongly Agree (Sangat Setuju)

---

---

> [!TIP]
> **Scoring Note:** 
> Sum items within each domain to establish construct scores for cluster input. High scores on ATT, SN, and PBC are predictive of higher BI, which in turn predicts B. This scale is highly suited for K-means or Latent Profile Analysis (LPA).
