\chapter{Expected Outcomes}

The execution of the comparative unsupervised clustering framework (K-Means, LPA, Two-Stage) is expected to successfully segment Malaysian caregivers into distinct, mathematically robust behavioral profiles based on their TPB constructs. The ultimate output of this research will be a \textbf{Targeted Virtual Counseling Model}. This clinical framework will allow therapists to administer the CSI-PASD survey and match a parent to the correct type of peer-support track based on their algorithmically determined cluster.
